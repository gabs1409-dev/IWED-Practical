About Saddle Sheek

For Equestrians, by Equestrians

Saddle Sheek is a niche equestrian brand dedicated to creating stylish, high-quality, and customisable apparel for both horse and rider. Our product range includes ear bonnets, numnahs, and baselayers, all designed with both performance and aesthetics in mind.

Built by equestrians, for equestrians, Saddle Sheek understands the needs of riders who want to combine functionality with style. Every product is created with attention to detail, ensuring comfort, durability, and a premium finish.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide equestrians with premium, customisable gear that enhances performance while allowing riders to express their personal style.

Our Values

  • Quality craftsmanship
  • Authenticity
  • Community-driven approach
  • Innovation in design
